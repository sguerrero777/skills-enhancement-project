# Skills Enhancement Project — EmporiUm Maryland Sales Analysis Version 2.0

## Overview

Sales Manager Shruti Reddy assigned a team of Data Analysts to perform a sales analysis for EmporiUm, a student bookstore chain that sells technology, textbooks, art supplies, 
and other products across the U.S. This analysis focuses on the Maryland territory and utilizes Excel and Power BI to target key business questions, identify trends, 
and deliver data-driven recommendations for the next quarter. 

This is Version 2.0 of Sharleen Guerrero's Capstone 1 sales territory analysis, built collaboratively as part of the Year Up DATA Skills Enhancement Project. The original analysis used SQL and Excel to examine EmporiUm's Maryland in-store sales data. Version 2.0 enhances those findings with interactive Power BI dashboards that allows the team to explore the data and communicate insights to a non-technical audience.

## Sales Territory Details
- Sales Manager: Shruti Reddy
- Regional Director: Michael Jarvis
- Assigned Territory: Maryland
- Region: Northeast
- Scope of Analysis: In-Store Sales for January 1, 2022 – December 31, 2025
- Online Sales is its own sales territory managed separately in the West region and is therefore excluded from this analysis.
  
## Executive Summary for Maryland
- Total Revenue $11,451,615.09 (Jan 2022 – Dec 2025)
- Regional Rank #1 Northeast Region (47.25% of regional revenue)
- Top Category Technology & Accessories ($7.8M; +$521,971 in 2025)
- Top Store: North Harford at $8,708,119 driven by transaction volume (69,530 transactions)
- At-Risk Category: Textbooks declined twice, in 2023 and again in 2025 (-$99,322)
- Growth Opportunity: Stationery & Supplies ranks #2 in transaction volume (9,862 transactions) despite only $102,807 in revenue
